What is Zero Waste?

Zero waste is an ideology, and a set of actionable principles focused on waste prevention. The goal of zero waste is for us to rethink of how we use our resources , it is to motivate us to move away from just throwing away our used goods which end up in incinerators. Instead for us to implement REFUSE , REDUCE , REUSE , RECYCLE , ROT concepts to reduce our impacts on our planet leading to a healthier earth.

The 5 R's of Zero Waste

Refuse

Decline what you don't need (single-use plastics, freebies, junk mail) to prevent waste at its source.

Reduce

Consciously lessen consumption and choose items with minimal packaging or longer lifespans.

Reuse

Opt for reusable items over disposable ones and find new purposes for old items to extend their utility.

Recycle

Properly sort and process materials that cannot be refused, reduced, or reused, turning them into new products.

compost

Compost organic materials like food scraps and yard waste, returning valuable nutrients to the soil.
